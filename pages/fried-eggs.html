FRIED EGGS WITH TOAST

INGREDIENTS

  • butter
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 or two slices of bread
  • cheese

STEPS

  1. heat some butter on pan to coat pan, low heat
  2. if scrambled eggs are desired, crack eggs in container, add a small amount of water, and beat thoroughly
  3. bread goes in the toaster sometime around here, the timing is tricky
  4. get egg guts on pan, turn up heat
  5. eggs will cook, prepare any additional food and watch toast
  6. when eggs start to become solid, i can use a spatula to move stuff around, if eggs aren't beet or an omelet effect is desired, I can flip the eggs in the pan
  7. as eggs start to finish i can add my cheese, and check my toast
  8. turn off heat when eggs aren't gross, keep in pan while toast finishes
  9. when toast is done, butter the toast, then pour cooked eggs onto toast for maximum enjoyment
