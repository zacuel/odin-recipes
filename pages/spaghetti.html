Spaghetti

INGREDIENTS

  • butter
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 or two slices of bread
  • cheese

STEPS

  1. boil water for noodles
  2. put noodles in boiling water, get water down to simmer
  3. start tomato sauce on low heat stir occasionally check if heated, get heated enough for meal
  4. when spaghetti melts in your mouth it is finished cooking
  5. pour spaghetti into strainer in sink
  6. mix sauce and noodles, serve on plates
