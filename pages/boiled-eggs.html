HARD BOILED EGGS

INGREDIENTS

  • 6 eggs
  • patience

STEPS

  1. Place eggs in pot
  2. cover eggs completely with water
  3. cover pot and turn stove on high
  4. bring water to a boil
  5. turn off heat and place pot on a cool burner, let sit for 15 minutes
  6. meanwhile, fill a large bowl with cold water
  7. after the 15 minutes, transfer the eggs to the cold water
  8. replace the cold water as needed until eggs are completely cooled
  9. let chill in the fridge at least 2 hours before peeling
